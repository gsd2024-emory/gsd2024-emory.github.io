COVID precautions for visitors

The safety of all event participants is of the utmost importance. To support Emory's safe and healthy environment, all visitors accessing campus are strongly encouraged to be vaccinated for COVID-19.

Emory University maintains the Emory Forward website with detailed information on COVID precautions. Within the Emory Forward site, please read the Visitor Policy page.

For visitors that are not Emory faculty, staff or students, you will note that the current policy (which can always change if community spread alters) allows visitors to access campus. We do ask that all visitors comply with the COVID-19 protocols and visitor guidelines listed on the visitor page.

Of particular note: Georgia Statistics Day will be a masked event. Please come prepared with your masks. With the current modified yellow status for the university, we are positioned to proceed with Georgia Statistics Day. Should this status change, we will keep all attendees updated and address at that time.
